20 GOTO 10 is a community hackathon celebrating retro technology.

The aim is to bring together those with fond memories of hacking away on their now-retro PCs, and younger programmers for a day of learning, building and sharing.

Key information

  • Date: Saturday October 19th 2019
  • Location: Ministry of Startups Paddington
  • Cost: free
  • Age requirements: 18+, however if you're taking part with a parent you may attend if you're 14+
  • Travel reimbursements: not this time, sorry
  • Overnight: No - one-day event, 10am-8pm

We need your support

We're aiming to make this event as awesome as possible, but to make that happen we need the following:

  • retro computers (in working order) and disks with relevant software
  • experts in using these old machines to volunteer as mentors
  • retro games consoles for our break-out space

If you can provide any of these, please contact the webmaster at 20goto10@underland.events
